Background

Motivation

Our motivation for creating this app came from our everyday observations that the popularity of social media and the widespread use of beauty filters have led to a significant rise in appearance anxiety and mental health challenges, especially among teenage girls. Excessive time spent on social media, photo editing, and plastic surgeries to meet others' definitions of beauty is all common symptoms of this unhealthy trend.

One source of the problem is conventional beauty filters, like Magic Mirrors from fairytales, all promote an external, unrealistic version of beauty. That's why we created Mindful Mirror to embrace an internal, healthy, and achievable definition of beauty.

Solution Strategy

We are at our Most Beautiful when we eat healthily, exercise regularly, sleep well, and feel peaceful and happy. The more people who embrace their own 'mindful beauty', the less peer pressure on social media to digitally enhance one's selfies to conform to an external unrealistic beauty standard. That's how we plan to combat appearance anxiety with Mimi.

Social Need

Our survey of 628 individuals in Nanjing (aged 15-40) highlighted the existence of widespread appearance anxiety, notably among females. Over 51% admitted to anxiety caused by beauty score apps, affecting 45.23% of males and 64.04% of females. Only 22% felt comfortable posting unfiltered selfies. This data indicates a substantial social demand for solutions like "Mindful Mirror" to combat the epidemic of appearance anxiety.

Product Introduction

Feature

Mindful Mirror uses a unique beauty score system to capture only health related features like BMI and sleep, regardless of the external standard that evaluate people's facial proportions etc.

Mission

Mindful Mirror's mission is to promote an alternative beauty standard based on mindfulness to combat appearance anxiety and improve health. Our mission statement is 'To help everyone embrace their healthy selves as the most beautiful.'

Service

Mindful Mirror is an app that promotes social benefits and the public good. All the services will be permanently free.

Technical Detail

Mindful Mirror was built in Android Studio, using Retrofit to connect with the backend server. The backend server uses the rest API interface based on the spring boot frame. The backend database uses MySQL. We created our app in Java, using API provided by TengineKit and codes from GitHub to detect facial features.

Impact

The Mindful Mirror App has received software copyright certification (2023SR1232532). In addition, Mimi was reported by national media ChinaLife on 10.20, in recognize of our innovation and positive impact on society.
